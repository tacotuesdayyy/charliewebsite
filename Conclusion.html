Conclusion and Apology

IQSSL has a short but rich history of events. Season 1 was a blast, Season 2 not so much, but iRacing has a funny way of creating memories throughout the chaos.

However, I wanted to issue an apology for my hishandled ownership towards the end of the season. My haste and aggrivation led me to ultimately cut the league a race short after all and without a proper finale.

I still feel regretful about it, however including this story while crafting this personal website helped give myself a sense of closure, as well as opening a window into the past for drivers interested in reading.

Thank you for taking the time to read a short history on a league that is a staple of my friend group.

-Charlie
